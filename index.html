EVENTS

Indie Unplugged , previously known as ‘Indie Lake’ is a two day acoustic jamming event that has grown in popularity, participation and footfall over the past years. Indie Unplugged is held in one of the most serene and beautiful places of the NIT Silchar campus where the Indie lake beautifies the ambience. It comprises of the two events, namely ‘Voice of Incand’ and Acoustic Jam’. The footfall of last year was around 1500 combining both the events.

Voice of Incand is a solo singing competition where participants try to hit the highest note and compete with each other for the coveted throne. The participants are given the choice of singing with help of karaoke, singing while playing an instrument or being accompanied by another musician. The participant pool consists of students from our college, noted singers from the town of Silchar and other musicians from the north eastern states.The singers are allowed to sing in all languages and genres. The venue becomes crowded with music lovers who come to enjoy a beautiful musical evening.

Acoustic Jam is the jamming event which takes place on the second day. It’s a group event that gives a platform for acoustic bands to showcase their talent and also get their hands on exciting prizes. The instruments allowed are acoustic guitar, bass guitar without amplifier, synthesizer and Cajon. The event has witnessed many bands who have given stellar performances by performing their own composition or making covers of famous and classic songs. The audience is enthralled as soon as a band starts playing an acoustic cover of their favorite rock song or a classic song that is close to the hearts of millions. The night sky and the vibrant decorations add to the heavenly atmosphere.
